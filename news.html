Holi !!

Date: March 10, 2025 - March 14, 2025

Location: KIET Entry Gate

Description: Holi is a vibrant Hindu festival celebrating the arrival of spring, love, and the victory of good over evil. It involves playing with colored powders, dancing, singing, and sharing festive foods. Families and communities come together to celebrate joy, forgiveness, and unity, marking the season of renewal.

Raksha Bandhan !!

Date: September 5, 2024 - October 10, 2024

Location: Our Home Worldwide

Raksha Bandhan is a traditional Hindu festival celebrating the bond between brothers and sisters. Sisters tie a protective rakhi (bracelet) on their brothers' wrists, symbolizing love and safety.
